love it if we made it

Conceptualized, developed, and completed during the second cycle of workshops. It is inspired by the song "Love It If We Made It" by The 1975. The poster aims to evoke the precarious anxiety conveyed by the rhythm of the words and the emphasis the singer places on the chorus. Fully functional on Artivive as it has been animated.
